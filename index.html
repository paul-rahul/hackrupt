78 days.  4 hours.  52 minutes.  29 seconds.

Register Now

  • Overview

    The world of finance is changing dramatically. Technology is influencing the financial services industry majorly. Tasks once handled with paper money, bulky computers, and human interaction are now being completed entirely on digital interfaces. Technology is transforming the way finance companies providers interact with their customers, just as it is also changing the way consumers’ access financial products. Technological influence on finance has never been greater at any point in human history. Consilium, the Business Conclave of NSIT, aims to bring together finance enthusiasts and scholars. The Conclave will engage the participants in a productive experience connecting and tampering with the finance sector. We at FES, the Finance and Economics Society of NSIT, aspire to be leaders in this fast growing and exciting field, and excited to have you join us in using technology to envision the future of finance and banking!

  • About the Event

    The finance and economics society of NSIT brings to you Hackrupt, a 24 hour FinTech hackathon to be held at NSIT aiming to merge the sectors of technology and finance by encouraging participants to create real world and usable software products as new innovative solutions to problems in the finance sector. We are looking for innovative and out of the box solutions which will revolutinize the Fintech sector.

    The event will be conducted just like traditional hackathons, a 24hr sprint of programming. Your goal should be to start from scratch and end with a working prototype modelling your solution. Themes of the hackathon will be released soon on the website. The solution could be anything from a website to a desktop software. The teams will have complete freedom on how they want to approach the idea, the only condition being that it should solve a problem or bring innovation in the field of finance. Advocating an ideology to recognize and reward innovation, Hackrupt offers exciting prizes to the winners. Participants will be judged in regular assessments throughout the 24 hours of the hackathon. Hackrupt includes all food and accommodation facilities for the participants. Register for a fun filled experience brainstorming with like minded people, with the bonus of an overnight coding spree.


Themes

Banking

Banks have changed in their operations and moved towards universal banking along with the increased usage of technology offering alternate channels such as smart cards, ATMs, usage of the internet, mobile and social banking. However, there are innumerable challenges that the banking sector faces today. Build novel and viable solutions using FinTech that will change the way every bank aspires.

Cryptocurrency

Do you have an idea for how cryptocurrency could be applied in a new way, or spread to new audiences? Come along for a day and build an application that adds value to the financial web. We are all open to unthinkable implementations or novel innovations.

Financial Inclusion

Financial inclusion refers to fintech solutions that provide more affordable finance alternatives to disadvantaged and low-income people who, like the unbanked/underbanked, may have little to no access to mainstream financial services. An estimated 2 billion working-age adults globally have no access to the types of formal financial services delivered by regulated financial institutions. With fintech, engineer a method to build solutions which reach every last potential consumer in need.

Stocks

There was a time when boisterous traders at the New York Stock Exchange yelled out orders to each other, creating a raucous din. Digital disruption made financial markets more transparent, accessible, inclusive and efficient. Ever thought of creating the next generation of Financial Markets Applications for Millenials? Join us and change the world of Trading and Investments.

Innovation

Innovation welcomes participants formulate novel finance sector solutions using emerging technologies. Participants may choose any subject they wish to present their ideas upon, open to a wide variety of online API tools at their disposal. Financial projects involving user interface, experience, market hacks are all welcome.
